Controls

Desktop

  • WASD — Move
  • Mouse — Look (click to lock)
  • Shift — Sprint
  • Space — Jump
  • Left Click — Attack (Plumbus)
  • Right Click / F — Plasma Rifle
  • 1 / 2 / 3 — Burps (Shouts)
  • E — Talk / Interact
  • Tab — Inventory & Quests
  • M — Map
  • Esc — Pause

Mobile

  • Left stick — Move
  • Drag right side — Look
  • ⚔ — Melee
  • 🔫 — Plasma
  • ⤒ — Jump
  • 🗣 1/2/3 — Burps
  • 👋 — Interact
  • ☰ — Menu
